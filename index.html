Earth is the third planet from the sun and the only known planet to harbor life, thanks to its liquid water, oxygen-rich atmosphere, and protective magnetosphere. It's a rocky, terrestrial planet with distinct internal layers—a solid inner core, a liquid outer core, a mantle, and a crust—and a surface covered by 71% water and 29% land. Earth orbits the sun in an oval-shaped orbit, completing one revolution in about 365 days, while also rotating on its axis to create a 24-hour day.
